Aerobic Capacity Training

Developing aerobic capacity is crucial for climbers, enhancing endurance on long routes and aiding in faster recovery during rests. It increases the efficiency of oxygen utilization in muscles, delaying fatigue and reducing the buildup of lactic acid. This is especially beneficial in maintaining consistent energy levels throughout a climb and ensuring effective recovery between attempts in bouldering. Ultimately, a higher aerobic capacity allows climbers to perform more effectively across various climbing disciplines, making it a key aspect of climbing fitness.
