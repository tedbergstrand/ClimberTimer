Aerobic Power Training

Aerobic power training enhances a climber's ability to perform at a higher intensity for a sustained period, crucial for extended challenging sections of a climb or a long boulder problem. It improves the rate at which oxygen is delivered to and is utilized by the muscles, allowing climbers to maintain a stronger pace without succumbing to fatigue quickly. This enhanced oxygen delivery is key for supporting sustained, strenuous climbing sections, enabling climbers to push through demanding parts of a route with greater efficiency. Additionally, improved aerobic power contributes to more effective recovery during short rests, helping climbers maintain their performance level throughout the climb.
